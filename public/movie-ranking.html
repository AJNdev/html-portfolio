The Best Movies According to Alex!

My top 3 movies of all-time

Your Name

My all time favourite movie, a well written romance movice about a boy living in Tokyo and a girl living in the country side swapping bodies. The movie is very well drawn and has made me cry several times.

Spiderman: into the spiderverse

Cool cross over film with various spidermen coming together, also introduces Miles.

Shutter Island

Thriller movie with a mind bending twist at the end!
